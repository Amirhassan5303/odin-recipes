Lasagna

How to Make Lasagna Step-By-Step

To make a simple lasagna, start by preparing the meat sauce. Brown ground beef in a pan with chopped onion and garlic, then drain excess fat. Add tomato sauce, salt, pepper, and a pinch of Italian seasoning, and let it simmer for 10–15 minutes. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese with an egg and a little salt.

Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). In a baking dish, layer cooked lasagna noodles, meat sauce, the ricotta mixture, and shredded mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers until ingredients are used, finishing with sauce and mozzarella on top. Sprinkle with grated Parmesan.

ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • ¾ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 12 lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions and set aside; brown ground beef with onion and garlic, then add tomato sauce and seasonings to make the meat sauce.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese with an egg and a pinch of salt until smooth.
  3. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C) and spread a thin layer of sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  4. Layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese, repeating the layers and finishing with sauce and cheese on top.
  5. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes, then uncover and bake 10–15 minutes more until bubbly; let rest briefly before serving.
